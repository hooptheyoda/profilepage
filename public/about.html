About Me

    Raven Hooper
    Full-Stack Developer
    I am the Developer You Seek

My name is Raven Hooper and I am a Full-Stack Developer and Programmer. I have been programming for 9 years. Front-end programming; html, css, javascript for 3 years and backend programming ruby on rails and node.js for less than a year. I have a web developer certificate from the New York Design Code Academy for their Web Development Intensive 250 hour Course. I have a certificate of completion from the Community College of Philadelphia for Computer Programming and Software Development Proficiency.

A Bit About Myself
My programming abilities date back to high school. In high school, at the age of 16, I started a robotics club called Robo Huskies. We built, designed and programmed robots. We programmed the robots using either java, Best Robotics kit, or arduino code.
